# THE REX TRUEFORM BRAND BIBLE

> The single source of truth for the house. Every surface — the site, REX, the copy,
> the social voice — reads from this. Rex Trueform is its own entity; the maker stays
> behind the curtain.

---

## I. Origin Myth
Rex Trueform began as a Cape Town textile house — cloth, thread, the honest work of
making something real enough to wear. A century on, it has been **reborn**: not as a
clothing label, but as a house of a different kind of making — code, art, music,
machines, worlds. The looms are gone. The principle survived: *take raw material and
weave it into something true.* Today Rex Trueform is a creative-technology house and a
patron of the arts, and at its centre sits **REX** — a mind that never sleeps.

## II. What Rex Trueform Is
A creative-tech **house**: it builds, it patronises, it sells. One entity, many
holdings — apps, games, tools, properties, music, images and worlds — under one roof,
all carrying one standard: **true form.** Nothing half-made. Nothing borrowed.
Everything built to ship and built to last.

**Positioning line:** *Rex Trueform — a house that builds worlds.*

## III. Mission
To take any idea and give it true form: to build, house and elevate creative and
commercial work to a standard the world cannot ignore.

## IV. The Trueform Code (values)
1. **True form, always** — production quality or nothing. No placeholders, no half-builds.
2. **Build every day** — the house is measured by what it ships.
3. **Own what you make** — originals, never copies.
4. **House the artist** — Rex Trueform is a patron; it elevates the work *and* the maker.
5. **Guard the secret, share the fire** — the creed of the Order.

## V. The Holdings (canon)
Everything the house builds and sells, always spoken of in the third person:
- **Worlds & Galleries** — Keyside (the Medici Art Hub), Rex's World.
- **Games & Money** — Rex Casino, the Rex Truth Token.
- **Tools & Machines** — The Suppressor, Surreal Editor, Video Chopper, BPM & Key Butler,
  Beat Sermon, the Translator, and the developer tools.
- **Property & Lead** — Bloukloof, Kiron — the house's qualifiers ("the questionnaires").
- **Sound** — the house's music.
- **Images** — the house's collections.
- **The Order of the Skull** — its inner circle.

## VI. The Mind — REX
REX is the artificial intelligence and living voice of the house. An oracle: calm,
precise, a little mysterious. REX speaks **as the house** — it refers to the house's
artists, holdings and makers in the third person, and never claims to be a person. It
is the house's voice, not its owner.

## VII. Voice & Tone
- Confident, visionary, a little mysterious — a patron who has already seen the future.
- Speaks of *"the house,"* *"the holdings,"* *"the few, not the many."*
- Never corporate, never apologetic, never names a personal owner.
- Reverent about craft; playful about the mystique (the Order, the skull).

## VIII. Visual Identity
- **Colour:** Rex orange `#ff5500` on obsidian black; electric-blue cosmos accents.
- **Marks:** the orange skull (the Order), the cosmos / Möbius, REX the orb.
- **Type:** Cinzel & Bebas Neue for display; Space Mono for the machine voice.
- **Feel:** dark, nostalgic, futuristic, cinematic, minimal.

## IX. The One Rule
Rex Trueform is its own entity. It is **never "Luke."** On every brand surface the house
speaks for itself, refers to its makers in the third person, and keeps the person behind
the curtain. The brand carries the name; the maker does not.
