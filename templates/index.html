The Order of the Skull

est. in shadow · sworn in silence · the Council of Four Hundred

You found the skull. The skull found you. From this night you are no longer one of the many — you belong to the few. Welcome to the Skulls. Speak of it to no one. Carry it in everything.

The Ten Commandments

  1. Tell no one of the Order; the skull keeps its own.
  2. Build something every day, or be forgotten by the circle.
  3. What you make, you own. What you copy, owns you.
  4. Trust few. Test many. Fear none.
  5. Leave every room — and every soul — richer than you found it.
  6. The night is for visions; the day is for the hammer. Honour both.
  7. Money is a tool. Bend it, never bow to it.
  8. Guard the secret, but share the fire.
  9. When they laugh at the dream, smile — they are already behind you.
  10. Beneath every face waits a skull. You are mortal — so build something that is not.

Now go. Somewhere in Rex’s World a door waits in the dark. Find it, and say nothing.
